## 반입 전략(Fetch)
- 요구 반입(Demand Fetch)
- 예상 반입(Anticipatory Fetch)
## 배치 전략(Placement)
- 최초 적합(First Fit)
- 최적 적합(Best Fit)
- 최악 적합(Worst Fit)
## 교체 전략(Replacement)
- OPT(OPTimal replacement, 최적교체)
- FIFO(First In First Out)
- LRU(Least Recently Used)
- LFU(Least Frequently Used)
- NUR(Not Used Recently)
- MRU(MFU, Most Recently Used/Most Frequently Used)
